About Us

SolvingLab

Solving Lab constitutes a pioneering, independent, private company of consulting services in the field of epidemiology and public health. Solving Lab is set up to solve as many equations as possible that will end in positive and beneficial outcomes. The main idea to set up Solving Lab was to tackle all the unresolved issues and problems that linger continuously among us, especially in regards to the epidemiology of various diseases and public health problems. Solving Lab’s main goal is to strengthen various scientific research, data collection, and analyses in order to deliver strong and useful results towards successful evidence-based interventions for addressing existing and rising problems globally.

Our Services

“Scientific Research, Data Collection & Analyses“

Quantitative Research

Designing descriptive as well as experimental quantitative research studies (i.e. cross sectional, cohort, clinical trials). Preparing all necessary research instruments like surveys, questionnaires and data collection tools. Executing research studies as per client’s requirements

Qualitative Research

Designing studies using participant observation, mixed methods, interviews, focus groups, case and content analysis; preparing research instruments like questionnaires, guides, and recording tools; executing site-based studies targeting participants per client requirements; and transcribing data for analysis.

In-depth and Simple Data Analyses

Monitoring and evaluation, preparing data analyses for all types of research data including existing secondary datasets, and conducting both in-depth and simple data analyses tailored to client preferences.

Detailed and Simplified Scientific Reports

Prepare detailed scientific reports aimed for specific scientific crowd as well as simplified scientific reports as well as non-scientific reports for the general or a neutral crowd.

Workshops and Trainings

Designing, planning, and executing workshops on epidemiology, public health, and health programs per client requirements, along with providing training and technical assistance for newcomers in these fields.

Programme Management

Programme management encompassing strategic project planning, continuous progress monitoring, efficient resource allocation and control, proactive risk management, comprehensive team mentoring, and the development and execution of effective strategies.

Dr. Gerald Marten

FREELANCE RESEARCH ASSOCIATE

Dr. Gerald Marten is an ecologist with fifty years of research experience in a variety of ecosystems around the world. He has lived and worked in the United States and Canada, Mexico and Central America, East Africa, Southeast Asia, and Japan. His innate love of nature, enriched by growing up in Southern California’s Santa Monica Mountains, kindled a scientific passion to explore how nature works. As his work progressed, it focused increasingly on practical ecological problems, and so did his commitment to translating science so people and communities can make good use of it. Author of Human Ecology: Basic Concepts for Sustainable Development (Earthscan Publications, 2001) and Traditional Agriculture in Southeast Asia: A Human Ecology Perspective (Westview Press, 1986), he has published approximately one hundred scientific articles, many of them on practical concerns such as fisheries and forest management, tropical agriculture and land use, alternative energy, ecological mosquito control, and how people can live sustainably on this planet. Four years ago he began the EcoTipping Points project to discover how lessons from environmental success stories can point the way to making a better world for his five grandchildren and all the rest of us.
